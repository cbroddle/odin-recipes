Green Bean Casserole

Description

A Thanksgiving favorite made from fresh ingredients instead of cans.

Ingredients

  • Fresh green beans
  • Cremini mushrooms
  • Butter
  • White Onion
  • Fresh parsley
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Sour cream
  • French fried onions

Steps

  1. Set oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cook green beans in boling water for 5 min. Drain and set aside.
  3. Melt butter in hot pan and add in cut onion and parsley. Cook about 5 min. Add flour and salt and pepper with onions. Whisk in milk, then add sour cream.
  4. Warm over medium heat until sauce thickens and bubbles. Add in cooked green beans and mushrooms, until coated with sauce.
  5. Remove from heat and add in cheese and some of the french fried onions. Leave some fried oonions for topping.
  6. Add mixture to casserole dish and top with cheese and rest of the fried onions.
  7. Bake 15-20 min, remove once cheese melted.
