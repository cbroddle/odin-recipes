Mashed Potatoes

Description:

Description here....

Ingredients:

  • Russet potatoes
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Salte and Pepper

Directions:

  1. Boil a pot of salted water. Cook potatoes for 15 min and drain.
  2. Heat butter and milk in a small pan on low heat.
  3. Mash cooked potatoes. Then, mix heated milk and butter with potatoes until desired texture is reached. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
